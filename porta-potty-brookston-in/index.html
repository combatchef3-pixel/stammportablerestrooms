Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a standard porta potty rental cost?

Average portable toilet rental prices range from $85–$130/week. Final cost depends on location, duration, and service frequency, especially in high-demand states like Texas or Florida.

Do you offer same-day delivery for emergencies?

Yes. Emergency temporary toilet rental is available with 4–8 hour response in most metro areas in California, Texas, Florida, Georgia, and Arizona.

Are your ADA units available for residential use?

Yes. ADA-compliant portable restroom rental is available for home events, medical needs, or accessibility requirements nationwide.

How often are flushable units serviced?

Standard service is once per week. High-use events may require twice-weekly cleanings to maintain freshwater and waste capacity.

Do handwashing stations require water hookups?

No. All portable toilets with handwashing stations are self-contained with onboard freshwater tanks, no external plumbing needed.
